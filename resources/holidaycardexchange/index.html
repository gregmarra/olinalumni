Holiday Card Exchange

TL;DR.

Spread some holiday cheer along with fellow alumni by sending greeting cards to one another during the holiday season.

more...

The Olin Holiday Gif Exchange is a simple and fun thing that alumni do to spread some Olin cheer amongst ourselves during the Holiday Season.

Here's how it works:

  • You fill out a Google Form, which includes how many cards you would like to give (which is also how many you’ll receive)!
  • We feed everyone's information into a super-awesome script written by Crawf ('09).
  • You get an email with a list of people to send cards, greetings, and happiness to!

You might go for the classic, the pretty, the weird, the handmade -- really anything goes as long as you don’t forget to send your cards!

FAQ

How long is the program? What is the time commitment?

You’ll be getting your matches in December and you have until late January to mail your cards.

How does participation work? Who can participate?

Alumni from 2006-2020 can participate in the program.

How will I get the addresses of the people I need to mail my cards to?

We will email you with the names and addresses of the people you will be mailing your greeting cards to.
